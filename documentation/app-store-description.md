## Description

**Birding, but make it fun.**

birdr turns bird watching into a real-world collection game. Snap a photo of any bird, and our AI identifies the species instantly. Every new species you discover unlocks a beautiful collectible card — complete with real conservation data, your photo, and the story of your first sighting.

Think of it as a Pokédex for real birds.

**SNAP & IDENTIFY**
See a bird? Take a photo. birdr's AI identifies the species in seconds — no field guide required. Whether you're in your backyard or on a nature walk, just point, shoot, and discover what you found. Works with 900+ North American bird species.

**BUILD YOUR COLLECTION**
Every species you identify becomes a collectible card in your personal bird museum. Each card features your photo, the date and location of your first sighting, conservation status, and species details. Watch your collection grow from your first backyard sparrow to rare warblers you never knew existed.

**EARN STREAKS & ACHIEVEMENTS**
Keep your daily streak alive by spotting a bird each day. Unlock achievements across four categories — collection milestones, streak tiers, family masters, and habitat masters. It's the outdoor hobby that rewards you for stepping outside.

**EXPLORE WHAT'S NEAR YOU**
Discover which species are in your area right now, filtered by season and region. Use the Explore tab as your personal field guide — see what's been spotted nearby and what's still waiting to be found. Your own sightings map tracks every bird you've photographed across the country.

**REAL BIRDS. REAL DATA.**
birdr is powered by real science, not guesswork. Every species includes accurate conservation status from the IUCN, habitat information, and distinguishing features. Your collection isn't just fun — it connects you to wildlife conservation in a way that matters.

**WHY BIRDR?**
Most birding apps feel like databases. birdr feels like a nature game — part scavenger hunt, part photo journal, part outdoor adventure. If you love geocaching, nature photography, or just want a reason to step outside, birdr makes every walk a chance to discover something new.

- AI bird identification from photos
- 900+ North American species to collect
- Collectible cards with real species data
- Daily streaks and achievement badges
- Explore species near you by season
- Your personal life list and sightings map
- Beautiful nature photography journal
- Wildlife conservation status on every card

**Start your collection. Can you spot them all?**

Free to start with 3 daily IDs. Upgrade for unlimited identification.
