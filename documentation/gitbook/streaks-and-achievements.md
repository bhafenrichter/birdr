# Streaks & Achievements

birdr rewards consistent birding and exploration with streaks and achievements. Here's how they work.

---

## Streaks

A streak tracks how many consecutive days you've captured at least one bird.

- **How it works:** Capture one or more birds each day to keep your streak alive. Miss a day and your streak resets to zero.
- **Where to see it:** Your current streak is displayed on your Profile tab, and a detailed view shows your current streak, longest streak, total birding days, and a 60-day calendar.
- **Streak reminders:** You can opt in to a daily push notification reminder if you haven't captured a bird yet that day.

### Streak Achievement Tiers

As your streak grows, you unlock streak achievements:

| Tier | Requirement |
|------|------------|
| Weekend Warrior | 3-day streak |
| Dedicated | 7-day streak |
| Committed | 14-day streak |
| Devoted | 30-day streak |
| Relentless | 60-day streak |
| Legendary | 100-day streak |

---

## Achievements

Achievements reward you for exploring different aspects of birding. There are four categories:

### Collection Milestones

Awarded for reaching species count thresholds in your collection. The more unique species you identify, the higher you climb.

### Streak Tiers

Awarded for maintaining consecutive daily capture streaks (see table above).

### Family Masters

Awarded for spotting a percentage of species within a bird family (e.g., warblers, hawks, sparrows). Tiers progress from Spotter to Master as you identify more species in each family.

### Habitat Masters

Awarded for spotting birds across different habitat types — forest, grassland, desert, wetland, freshwater, coast, mountain, tundra, and urban. Explore different environments to unlock these.

---

## Viewing Your Achievements

Go to **Profile > Achievements** to see all your achievements, including:

- Unlocked achievements with the date you earned them.
- In-progress achievements with your current progress.
- Locked achievements you haven't started yet.
