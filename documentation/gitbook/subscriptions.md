# Subscriptions

birdr offers both a free tier and paid subscription plans.

---

## Free Tier

Every birdr user gets a limited number of bird identifications per day at no cost. This is a great way to get started and see if birdr is right for you.

When you reach your daily limit, you'll need to wait until midnight (local time) for your quota to reset, or upgrade to a paid plan for unlimited identifications.

---

## Paid Plans

Upgrading to a paid subscription gives you **unlimited daily bird identifications** so you never miss a capture.

| Plan | Price |
|------|-------|
| Weekly | $3.99 / week |
| Annual | $29.99 / year |

The annual plan offers significant savings compared to the weekly plan.

---

## Managing Your Subscription

### How to subscribe

You can subscribe from within the app when you reach your daily limit, or anytime from **Profile > Subscription**.

### How to cancel

Subscriptions are managed through your device's app store:

- **iOS:** Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > birdr
- **Android:** Google Play Store > Payments & Subscriptions > Subscriptions > birdr

Canceling stops future renewals. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

### Refunds

Refund requests are handled by Apple or Google, depending on your platform. Visit their support pages for assistance.

---

## What You Keep

If you cancel your subscription, you keep everything in your collection. Your bird cards, sightings, streaks, and achievements are yours. You'll simply return to the free tier's daily identification limit.
